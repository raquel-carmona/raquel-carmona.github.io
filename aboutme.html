Sobre mi

Hi, I'm Raquel Carmona, graphic designer originally from Cordoba, Spain. I'm a creative dedicated to the artistic and cultural sector, with special interest in the development of visual identities, editorial and web design.

Raquel Carmona, 2023

Curious, active, passionate, sociable, and empathetic both personally and professionally. I have always been attracted to learning and creating. I see design everywhere as a method of expression and where I feel free working. I like digital work, technology, and the fusion of languages. I love working in a team, I am not afraid of making mistakes and failing, I seek to surround myself with people who inspire me and enrich ourselves mutually.

Biografía

During my studies in Fine Arts, I developed a passion for engraving, linocut, and woodcut. Exploring these techniques merged my interest in drawing with my interest in design. I began to delve into the image laboratory, where I discovered new printing methods and completed my Final Degree Project. Essentially, I learned to use the machinery autonomously and experimentally, focusing on self-publishing and risography. What I enjoyed the most was the freedom to mix graphic languages, merging digital with manual. Afterward, it was clear to me that I wanted to continue working in the world of graphic design.
